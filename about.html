Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves access to quality financial education and personalized guidance to achieve their dreams.

Our Story

Built to Bridge the Financial Literacy Gap

Rise was born from a simple observation: too many hardworking people struggle with their finances not because they lack discipline, but because they've never had access to proper financial education.

We partnered with credit unions who share our vision—institutions that genuinely care about their members' financial wellbeing, not just their account balances.

Today, Rise connects thousands of members with certified financial coaches, interactive educational resources, and tools that make financial planning accessible to everyone.
