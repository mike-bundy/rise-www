🏆 Round 4 Recommendations

After Round 4 research, the ranking tightens to one strong new entrant plus the three carried-over top picks.

  1. RainCents — still the strongest overall. All 3 domains free, zero brand conflicts, clean trademark path, great PNW story.
  2. SproutBux — tied for second. All 3 domains free, zero conflicts, fun and memorable.
  3. SproutKash — third. All 3 domains free, no conflicts, slightly edgier than SproutBux thanks to the K-spelling.
  4. Centscope — new in Round 4. All 3 domains free, no fintech competitor, no USPTO hit. The only Round 4 name that clears every bar. Tonally adjacent to "periscope" — suggests clarity and observation, which fits a financial-visibility product.
  5. FinCoachIQ — new in Round 4, with caveats. All 3 domains free, but lives inside a saturated "FinCoach" brand space. Workable if we're willing to own the disambiguation work.
❌ Eliminated in Round 4: FinCoach (8+ active brands + all domains gone), ProsperIQ (active nonprofit in Rise's mission space), CashCompass (three live budgeting apps + all domains), FinCompass (all domains gone), FinVoyage (active Google Play investment app), Navify (Roche Diagnostics' registered global brand), WealthWayfinder (live finance-content site), WealthTrekker ("Wealth" mismatches Rise positioning), Waylytics (wrong category register), Levara (all 3 standard TLDs gone), Ascenda (global fintech loyalty provider), Ascensia (registered USPTO TM, diabetes care), Riko (premium legacy domains), Prosperon (direct competitor in college financial education).
