🏆 Round 3 Recommendations

Given the Round 3 results, the field narrows sharply.

  1. RainCents — still the strongest overall. All 3 domains free, zero brand conflicts, clean trademark path, great PNW story. This is the easiest to ship.
  2. SproutBux — tied for second. All 3 domains free, zero conflicts, fun and memorable. Different tonal register from RainCents (playful vs. poetic) — picks your target audience.
  3. SproutKash — third, same clean profile. All 3 domains free, no conflicts. Slightly edgier than SproutBux thanks to the K-spelling.
  4. GrowCents — fallback. Clean of fintech brand conflicts but growcents.com is already registered and hosting something. Workable on .org/.net only.
  5. NW Wallet — regional fallback. Clear and descriptive but .com gone and the PNW focus caps national ambition.
❌ Eliminated in this round: KashLab (KashLab Pte Ltd Singapore fintech, correction from Round 1), Kashly (two active apps), Finovo (three active fintechs), Coinsprout (crypto newsletter + fintech grantee), VivaWallet ($800M JPMorgan-backed European unicorn), Prospera (live USPTO TM + multiple fintechs), Solaro and Crecio (no fintech TM collision but all domains gone).

💡 Domain availability verified via direct WHOIS against the authoritative registries (Verisign for .com/.net, Public Interest Registry for .org) on April 10, 2026. Confirm with your registrar at purchase time — fresh registrations happen daily.
