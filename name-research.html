Pattern observation across 7 rounds: invented compounds on a money-stem suffix (–Cents, –Bux, –Kash) or distinctive natural roots paired with money stems (Cedar–, Cove–, Rain–) keep finding clean ground. Single dictionary words, given names, surnames, and place names all hit walls — a 1990s/2000s .com holder plus an active credit union running a financial-wellness program. Avoid -Coin (crypto), -Stash (neobank), -Vault (savings TM overlap), and any compound that stacks two crowded financial roots.
Domain availability verified via direct WHOIS against the authoritative registries (Verisign for .com/.net, Public Interest Registry for .org).
